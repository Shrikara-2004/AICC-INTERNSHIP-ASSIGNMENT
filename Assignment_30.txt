>Detects suspicious activities or unknown persons in restricted areas.
>Attendance Systems
>Automatically marks attendance using face recognition in schools or offices.
>Autonomous Vehicles
>Detects pedestrians, vehicles, and obstacles to ensure safe driving.
>Retail Stores
>Tracks customer movement and behavior for better store management.
>Designed Solution: Smart Attendance System

Problem:
Manual attendance is time-consuming and can lead to errors or proxy attendance.

Solution:
A face detection-based attendance system that automatically records attendance.

How it Works:

A camera captures student faces in real-time
The system detects and recognizes faces using trained models
If a match is found, attendance is marked automatically
Data is stored in a database for records

Expected Output:

>Accurate attendance records
>Reduced manual effort
>Faster and secure attendance system
