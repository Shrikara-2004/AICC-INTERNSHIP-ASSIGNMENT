Assignment: Real-Life AI Applications Used Daily
10 Real-Life AI Applications & How ML Works Behind Them

1️⃣ YouTube Recommendations
Machine learning models analyze your watch history, likes, and viewing time to predict and recommend videos you’re likely to watch next.

2️⃣ Google Search
ML ranks search results by understanding intent, keywords, and past user behavior using ranking algorithms and NLP models.

3️⃣ Instagram Feed
AI analyzes posts you interact with (likes, shares, comments) and predicts what content should appear higher in your feed.

4️⃣ Google Maps Traffic Prediction
ML models analyze real-time GPS data from users to predict traffic congestion and suggest faster routes.

5️⃣ Email Spam Filter (Gmail)
Supervised learning models classify emails as spam or not spam based on patterns learned from labeled data.

6️⃣ Voice Assistants (Siri / Google Assistant)
Speech recognition models convert voice to text, and NLP models understand intent and generate appropriate responses.

7️⃣ Netflix Recommendations
Collaborative filtering predicts movies/shows based on similar users’ preferences and viewing patterns.

8️⃣ Amazon Product Recommendations
AI analyzes purchase history, browsing behavior, and similar users to suggest products you might buy.

9️⃣ Face Unlock on Smartphones
Computer vision models detect and compare facial features using deep learning to authenticate users.

🔟 Online Payment Fraud Detection
ML models detect unusual transaction patterns and flag suspicious activities in real-time.
